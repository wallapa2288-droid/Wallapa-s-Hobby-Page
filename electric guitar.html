Step-by-Step: How to Begin Playing Guitar

  1. Tune your strings properly.
  2. Set up your amplifier and cables.
  3. Adjust your effects pedals and tone.
  4. Warm up with chords and scales.
  5. Practice a beginner-friendly song or riff.
